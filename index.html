How Wander works

Frictionless wandering through multi-signal input.

Gestures release you from the friction of keyboard and mouse. Instead of translating intent into clicks and typing, you can speak, point, and move naturally. Facial expressions open another layer beyond prompts, helping Wander read intention more like we do when talking with other humans.

Voice gives the explicit direction. The pill shows what Wander heard and what it applied.
Face can carry intent beyond words. Example: "add rain" + a smile may become "add rain, with a hopeful mood."
Gestures remove the need to describe everything with a cursor or keyboard. Point, say "add a car here," and "here" becomes that spot.

If live stream slots fill up, older cards loop recent motion instead of going blank.
